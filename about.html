About Me

I am Saranya Satheesh, a passionate designer dedicated to creating meaningful, inclusive, and impactful user experiences. With a deep appreciation for research-driven design, I focus on understanding people’s needs and behaviors to develop solutions that are both accessible and empowering.

I strongly believe that design should be inclusive and representative of diverse voices. That’s why I incorporate research methodologies that emphasize co-creation, empathy, and appreciation—ensuring that users are not just subjects of study but active participants in the design process.

My Journey

I started my journey in design with a deep curiosity about how people interact with the world. Through research and user-centered design, I aim to create solutions that are thoughtful and impactful.
